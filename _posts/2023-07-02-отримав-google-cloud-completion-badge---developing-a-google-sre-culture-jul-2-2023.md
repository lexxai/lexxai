#### Developing a Google SRE Culture

In many IT organizations, incentives are not aligned between developers,
who strive for agility, and operators, who focus on stability. Site
reliability engineering, or SRE, is how Google aligns incentives between
development and operations and does mission-critical production
support. Adoption of SRE cultural and technical practices can help
improve collaboration between the business and IT. This course
introduces key practices of Google SRE and the important role IT and
business leaders play in the success of SRE organizational adoption.
